About me

My name is Albert Ahn. I am currently working as an Emergency Medical Technician (EMT) at Allina Health, licensed in the state of Minnesota. I bring over two years of experience in pre-hospital care, including my time with a non-transport BLS service at Macalester College, where I also served in a leadership capacity to support team readiness and certification compliance.

While I have a strong interest in emergency medical services, I am also pursuing my long-term goal of becoming a professional pilot. I am currently preparing to begin flight training, while also considering paramedic school as a way to further develop my skills in the EMS field.

 In addition to my work in healthcare, I am expanding my technical skill set through coursework in HTML and web fundamentals. I see value in integrating a range of skills—clinical, technical, and operational—as I continue to grow professionally.

 I recently graduated with a Bachelor of Arts from Macalester College (Class of 2025, pending final credit completion) and am currently focused on settling into my new role at Allina Health.
